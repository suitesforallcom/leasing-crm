This Privacy Policy describes how Kiwi Rentals, LLC (“Kiwi Rentals,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information in the course of operating our rental-property business. We use an internal property-management web application called SuitesForAll, hosted at https://suitesforall.web.app (the “Service”), to manage our buildings, leases, billing, and tenant communications.

The Service is internal software built and operated by Kiwi Rentals for our own use. We do not license, sell, or offer the Service to other landlords, property managers, or third parties. This Policy therefore covers personal information about:

  • Tenants and prospective tenants of properties owned or managed by Kiwi Rentals.
  • Authorized users of the Service — Kiwi Rentals owners, employees, and contractors who sign in to manage operations.
  • Other individuals whose information enters the Service in the normal course of our rental business (for example, emergency contacts, guarantors, or counterparties on a payment).
Quick summary. Kiwi Rentals collects what we need to manage our rental properties and our tenants’ leases and payments. We do not sell personal information and we do not use it for advertising. Payments are processed by Stripe; e-signatures by DocuSign; bank-account linking (when used) by Stripe Financial Connections. You have the right to access, correct, delete, and export your personal information — see Section 9.

2. Information we collect

a) Tenant and prospective-tenant information

  • Name, company name (where applicable), email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Lease terms: start date, end date, suite or unit number, rent amount, security deposit, and the executed lease document (PDF, when DocuSign is used).
  • Billing data: invoice amounts, payment status, payment dates, payment-method last four digits and brand (received from Stripe), late-fee history, and outreach notes.
  • Optional notes, photos, and attachments stored against the tenancy record (for example, move-in inspection photos).
  • Emergency contact and guarantor information, where you have provided it.

b) Authorized-user account information

  • For Kiwi Rentals owners, employees, and contractors who sign in to the Service: name, work email address, phone number, and the workspace role assigned to the account (admin, manager, map editor, viewer).
  • Authentication identifiers (Firebase user ID, sign-in timestamps, IP address used for sign-in).

c) Payment information

When we send an invoice or a tenant pays online, payment-method information (card number, bank account details, billing address) is collected and processed directly by Stripe. Kiwi Rentals never receives, stores, or transmits raw card numbers or full bank-account credentials. We receive from Stripe only metadata such as the Stripe customer ID, invoice ID, payment status, last four digits of the payment instrument, and brand or network.

d) Bank-feed information (Kiwi Rentals business accounts only)

When we connect one of our own business bank accounts to the Service via Stripe Financial Connections, we receive (subject to our consent at the time of linking): the financial institution name, masked account number (last four), account type, account holder name (Kiwi Rentals), current and available balances, and a feed of incoming and outgoing transactions on that business account. We use this data solely to match incoming deposits to outstanding rent invoices in our system. Tenants’ personal bank accounts are never connected through this feature. See Section 6 for details.

e) Communications

  • Emails we send through the Service (invoices, reminders, lease documents) and their metadata (subject, recipient, send timestamp, delivery status).
  • Tenant outreach log entries (calls, emails, notes) recorded internally by our team.

f) Technical and usage information

  • Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and language reported by the device used to access the Service.
  • IP address and approximate location (city or region) derived from it.
  • Pages and features accessed inside the Service, timestamps of actions, and error events.
  • Locally stored data in the user’s browser (see Section 13).

g) Building, unit, and floor-plan information

The Service stores property data we maintain about our buildings: addresses, floor plans (uploaded as images or PDFs), unit dimensions and layouts, photos, and operational notes. To the extent any of this data identifies an individual (for example, a tenant’s name on a unit), it is covered by this Policy.

6. Bank-account connections (Stripe Financial Connections)

To reconcile rent payments efficiently, Kiwi Rentals connects its own business bank account(s) to the Service using Stripe Financial Connections. The linking flow is operated by Stripe. When an authorized Kiwi Rentals user clicks Connect bank account in Settings → Integrations → Bank Connections:

  1. Stripe’s widget opens in the browser.
  2. The user selects the financial institution and authenticates using the bank’s own login.
  3. The bank shows exactly which permissions are being granted (transactions, balances, account ownership).
  4. On success, Stripe issues a token that lets the Service read transactions and balances for the linked Kiwi Rentals account(s) only.

What we do with the data:

  • Periodically poll the connected business account for new transactions (typically every several hours).
  • Match incoming deposits to outstanding rent invoices in our system.
  • Display the matched payment status and (optionally) the current account balance to authorized Kiwi Rentals personnel.

What we do not do:

  • We do not initiate withdrawals, transfers, or any movement of money out of the connected account through this connection.
  • We do not link any tenant’s personal bank account through this feature — only Kiwi Rentals’ own business accounts.
  • We do not share the bank-feed data with anyone outside Kiwi Rentals and Stripe.
  • We do not use the data for credit decisioning, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to invoice reconciliation.

Revocation. Kiwi Rentals can revoke access at any time by clicking Disconnect on the connected account in Settings → Integrations → Bank Connections, directly from the bank’s online banking portal, or from the Stripe dashboard. Revocation stops all future data retrieval; data already pulled is retained according to Section 8.

Stripe’s end-user disclosures for Financial Connections (which describe Stripe’s independent role and obligations) are available at stripe.com/legal/end-users.

8. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as is necessary to operate our rental business and to comply with legal obligations. In practice:

  • Authorized-user account data — for as long as the user has access, plus 90 days after deactivation (to allow restoration from backups).
  • Tenant records — for the duration of the tenancy plus a retention window required by applicable landlord-tenant and tax law (often 7 years in the United States).
  • Payment records — retained for at least 7 years to satisfy financial-record-keeping obligations.
  • Bank-feed transactions — retained for the active period of the bank connection plus 24 months, after which they are aggregated or deleted.
  • Backups — daily snapshots are kept for at least 30 days; manual backups for at least 90 days.
  • Audit logs — retained for at least 12 months.

We may delete or anonymize specific tenant records earlier on request, subject to legal-retention exceptions, by using the in-app Erase Tenant PII action or by contacting us at the email below.

13. Cookies and local storage

The Service uses the browser’s localStorage and IndexedDB (not classic HTTP cookies) to store sign-in state, last-used workspace settings, draft form data, and integration credentials voluntarily entered by an authorized user. Firebase Authentication uses cookies set by Google for the OAuth and session-management flow.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics that profile individual users for marketing purposes. Local browser storage may be cleared at any time via the browser’s settings; doing so will sign the user out of the Service.

14. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including:

  • HTTPS / TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Encryption at rest in Google Cloud infrastructure.
  • Role-based access control inside the Service; principle of least privilege.
  • API secrets stored exclusively in Google Cloud Secret Manager and never embedded in client code.
  • Stripe webhook signature verification on every inbound event.
  • Daily encrypted backups with retention.
  • Audit logging of sensitive actions (deploys, deletions, financial events).

No system is perfectly secure. If you become aware of a security issue, please report it to finance@kiwi-rentals.com.
